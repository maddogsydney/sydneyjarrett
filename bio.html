Sydney Jarrett is an artist working on Gadigal land. His multi‑disciplinary practice explores re‑compositions of materialities of the state. They are engaged in practices of loitering and gestures of refusal.

They are currently completing their Honours in Fine Arts at UNSW Art & Design.
